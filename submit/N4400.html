Concurrency TS Editor's Report, April 2015

Overview

As of April 10, 2015, the last approved working draft of the Concurrency TS is N4107.

N4399 is the new proposed working draft of the Concurrency TS.

History

Below is the summary of changes that went into N4399:

  • N4123 proposed improvements to the Concurrency Technical Specification. These changes were approved with comments by SG1 and LEWG in Urbana, and incorporated into N4313.
  • In Cologne, N4313 was reviewed by LWG and approved with suggestions. These suggestions (tracked by https://github.com/cplusplus/concurrency_ts/issues) were then incorporated into N4399.
  • Also in Cologne, LWG reviewed N4204. The authors of N4204 incorporated the LWG feedback into N4392. That paper has been submitted to the Lenexa mailing and also merged into N4399.
  • Additionally, N4399 incorporates a portion of N4260 that was reviewed by SG1 and LWG in Urbana.

The full history of changes can be traced at https://github.com/cplusplus/concurrency_ts.
